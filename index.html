I work on AI for medical imaging—especially dermatology—using GAN-based image translation (CycleGAN, CUT, UNIT) and KAN-enhanced modules. My current research focuses on clinical↔dermoscopic translation with quantitative evaluation (FID, LPIPS) and classifier consistency checks. I also build anomaly detection pipelines (LSTM-AE, Deep-SVDD) and apply XAI methods (e.g., SHAP) to security and healthcare problems. I care about simple, reproducible PyTorch pipelines and clean academic artifacts (papers, posters, demos).
