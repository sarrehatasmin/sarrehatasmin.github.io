Hello! I am currently pursuing my M.S. in Information and Communication Engineering at Sun Moon University where I am advised and funded by Prof. Wonsang You.

My research interests lie in Explainable Artificial Intelligence (XAI), Generative Adversarial Networks (GANs), and Medical Imaging. I focus on developing interpretable and efficient deep learning frameworks that enhance transparency and diagnostic reliability in healthcare AI. My current work includes clinical-to-dermoscopic image translation, CycleGAN-based medical data augmentation, and KAN-based explainable modeling for rare skin diseases.

My broader goal is to contribute to trustworthy, human-centered AI in the medical domain, bridging the gap between computational models and clinical understanding.
